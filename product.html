Tabletop Zen Garden

Bring peace and focus to your desk, home, or workspace with our handcrafted Tabletop Zen Garden.

Inspired by traditional Japanese karesansui gardens, this compact set lets you create your own miniature landscape — perfect for moments of relaxation and mindfulness during a busy day.

What's Included

  1. Wooden Tray - Smooth, natural finish for a warm, organic feel.
  2. Fine White Sand - Soft texture for effortless raking and shaping.
  3. Polished Stones - Each one unique, symbolizing mountains or islands.
  4. Mini Rake - Create ripples, patterns, and flowing lines to suit your mood.
  5. Moss Accent - Adds a touch of natural green for balance and harmony.
