About

Welcome to Zunaid's Zen Zone - a digital space inspired by the beauty and simplicity of traditional Japanese Zen gardens.

Zen gardens, or karesansui, are more than just sand, stones, and carefully placed elements. They are spaces for reflection, mindfulness, and appreciating the art of simplicity. Every ripple in the sand, every stone's position, is deliberate — inviting you to slow down, breathe, and be present.

This project is part of The Odin Project curriculum, built to practice HTML, CSS, and flexbox principles. While it's a learning exercise, it's also a creative outlet — combining code with the calming philosophy of Zen.

Whether you're here to explore, to learn, or to simply enjoy the peaceful visuals, I hope you find a moment of stillness in this little corner of the web.
